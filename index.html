Hi! My name is Phat. When I'm 14 years old, I have been to the most beautiful waterfall in the world. This trip is Tee Lor Su waterfall. It is the most beautiful waterfall that I have ever seen for the first time in my life. I'm sure you will get a surprise once in your life. This waterfall is located in Umphang District, Tak Province. Tee Lor Su waterfall is reputed to be the most beautiful waterfall in Thailand and Tee Lor Su waterfall in karen language, it means black waterfall. Tee Lor Su waterfall not only a waterfall and back, but there is also a place to stay for camping and it's good for sleeping under the stars and enjoying the nature, but if anyone doesn't like to pitch a tent at this place there are resorts as well and there are also activities such as rafting. I can say that it's really beautiful as people say and it's worth coming to the Tee Lor Su waterfall
